Million Hearts Risk Check Challenge

Know Your Heart is the next step in using web technology to educate consumers about their health. Developed by XLBao Lab, it is a web-based and mobile app that calculates a user’s risk of developing cardiovascular disease (CVD) using a modeling tool built by Archimedes.

Know Your Heart is accessible to anyone with an internet connection. It is a free platform-agnostic app that can be accessed on mobile devices and personal computers. It can be downloaded as a standalone app or accessed through a web browser at http://KnowYourHeart.info. We currently support Android devices. Smartphone use is growing and the ability to access the app immediately after an initial marketing impression is important. However, since many Americans also use personal computers, we also provide a desktop browsing experience.

The app guides the user and encourages him to take action to improve his health. CVD is a serious condition and the user needs to be comfortable and feel like the app is trustworthy. The overall experience is simple, with each word carefully chosen and each technical word explained. The user can complete the initial risk assessment from beginning to end in less than two minutes. Lastly, the user should be confident that the app is giving an accurate prediction. We put front and center the credibility and sophistication of the Archimedes IndiGO model.
