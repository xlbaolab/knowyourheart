Did you know?

Back

1 in 3 adults in the U.S. have some form of heart disease, stroke, or other blood vessel diseases

And that the top risk factors for heart disease and stroke are:

  1. Inactivity
  2. Obesity
  3. High Blood Pressure
  4. Cigarette Smoking
  5. High Cholesterol
  6. Diabetes

Know Your Heart can help you take steps to lower that risk today!

Some things you should know before you go

  • Blood pressure tests are usually free.
  • Cholesterol tests usually cost about $35. Be sure to request a Full Lipid Panel that includes: Total Cholesterol, HDL, and LDL.
  • For best results, do not eat 9-12 hours before a cholesterol test.

Back

How many hours do you spend doing vigorous physical activities in a typical week?

Vigorous activities are ones that cause heavy sweating or large increases in breathing or heart rate for at least 10 minutes at a time.

e.g. running, lap swimming, aerobics classes, or fast bicycling
