Screening Tips

Your Heart
  • Many pharmacies and clinics offer in-store health tests that measure blood pressure and cholesterol values.
  • BLOOD PRESSURE TEST: FREE

  • Procedure:
    Blood pressure monitor is wrapped around your arm.
    The wrap is tightened and your blood pressure is measured with a device or stephoscope

  • Result:
    Two numbers will be given: systolic and diastolic .

  • CHOLESTEROL TEST: about $35

  • Request a Full Lipid Panel that includes: Total Cholesterol, HDL, and LDL

  • For best results, do not eat 9-12 hours before cholesterol testing

  • Procedure:
    Finger is pricked and blood sample is taken
    The blood is then analyzed by a lab machine

  • Result:
    At least three numbers will be given: Total Cholesterol, HDL, and LDL

  • TOTAL TIME: less than 30 minutes

Vigorous Activity

How many hours do you spend doing vigorous physical activities in a typical week?

Vigorous activities are ones that cause heavy sweating or large increases in breathing or heart rate for at least 10 minutes at a time.

For example: running, lap swimming, aerobics classes, or fast bicycling
