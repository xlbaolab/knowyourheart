Screening Tips

Back

Many pharmacies and clinics offer in-store health tests that measure blood pressure and cholesterol values.

- Blood Pressure tests are usually free

- Request a Full Lipid Panel that includes: Total Cholesterol, HDL, and LDL

- For best results, do not eat 9-12 hours before cholesterol testing

- Cost: about $35 without a coupon
